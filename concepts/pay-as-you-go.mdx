AgentKey bills purely on usage. There are no monthly platform fees, no minimum
spend, and no per-provider subscriptions — your agent's calls draw down a single
shared **credit balance**.

## How it works

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="One balance" icon="wallet">
    A single credit balance covers every service your agent uses.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Per-call pricing" icon="tag">
    Each tool has a known credit cost, surfaced when you describe it.
  </Card>
  <Card title="One invoice" icon="receipt">
    No surprise caps or overages across dozens of vendors.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## See the cost before you spend

Every `find_tools` and `describe_tool` result includes the per-call cost in
credits. Before any bulk work, multiply that cost by your planned call count, and
check your balance with the free [`account`](/api-reference/account) tool.

<Tip>
  For work involving several calls or a meaningful credit estimate, confirm the
  plan and cost with the user before proceeding.
</Tip>

## Managing your balance

Top up and review usage from the [console](https://console.agentkey.app). The
`account` tool returns your remaining credits and upstream health at any time —
and never costs credits to call.

<Note>
  Exact credit-to-USD pricing and per-tool costs are shown in the console and in
  each tool's describe output.
</Note>
