The **Social** category is AgentKey's largest — over **1,100 tools** spanning 19
platforms. It lets agents monitor trends, sentiment, conversations, and creator
data in real time.

<Tip>
  Social has many endpoints per platform. Discover with `find_tools` using the
  platform name and your intent, e.g. `find_tools("trending posts on X about GPT")`,
  or browse with `list_tools("social")`.
</Tip>

## Platforms

| Region | Platforms |
| --- | --- |
| Global | X/Twitter, Reddit, YouTube, LinkedIn, TikTok, Instagram, Threads, Lemon8 |
| China | Douyin (抖音), Zhihu (知乎), Bilibili, Weibo (微博), Xiaohongshu / Red Book (小红书), WeChat (微信), Kuaishou (快手), Pipixia (皮皮虾) |

## Typical uses

- **Brand & competitor monitoring** — track mentions and sentiment on X, Reddit, Weibo.
- **Trend discovery** — trending topics, hashtags, and viral content on TikTok, Douyin, Bilibili.
- **Content extraction** — YouTube transcripts, post text, and creator/channel data.
- **Research** — community discussion from Reddit, Zhihu, and Xiaohongshu.

## Example

```text
find_tools("what people are saying about AgentKey on X today")
describe_tool("<returned Provider/Operation>")
execute_tool("<Provider/Operation>", { ...params })
```

<Note>
  Endpoints, params, and per-call costs differ widely across platforms. Always
  `describe_tool` before executing.
</Note>
